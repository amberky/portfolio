About me

I am a full stack web developer with a passion for creating interactive and responsive web applications.

My journey into the world of coding began in 2009 when I started creating Google Blogger themes. This initial step quickly turned into a passion for coding and today, I am a software engineer with over 6 years of experience.

I thrive on building applications from scratch with a particular enthusiasm for when engineering and design come together to create seamless user experiences. My work is driven by the belief that technology should enhance and simplify the user's life.

Throughout my career, I've learned the value of work-life harmony and the benefits of remote work, which allow for greater flexibility and productivity. I am especially interested in working with medium-sized companies that uphold these principles and foster a collaborative environment. I invite you to explore my portfolio. If my work resonates with you, I would love to connect and discuss how we can work together.
